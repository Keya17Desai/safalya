SAFALYA GROUP : ABOUT US

“SAFALYA”, A SANSKRIT TERM THAT MEANS DESIRED SUCCESS EFFICACIOUSLY ACCOMPLISHED AND IT APTLY REFLECTS OUR SPIRITS, ATTITUDE AND OBJECTIVES.

SAFALYA GROUP IS DEDICATED TO WORKING COLLABORATIVELY WITH OUR CLIENTS, ARCHITECTS, AND CONTRACTORS TO BRING THEIR VISION TO LIFE. OUR AIM IS TO EMERGE AND EVOLVE AS THE MOST PROMISING, RELIABLE AND RESPECTABLE DEVELOPER. WE BELIEVE IN A TRANSPARENT AND HONEST APPROACH THAT FOSTERS TRUST AND LONG-TERM RELATIONSHIPS. WE ARE COMMITTED TO USING THE HIGHEST QUALITY MATERIALS AND IMPLEMENTING THE LATEST TECHNOLOGY AND INNOVATIONS TO ENSURE THAT EVERY HOME WE BUILD IS OF EXCEPTIONAL QUALITY AND CRAFTSMANSHIP, AND THAT EVERY CONSTRUCTION OF OURS IS A CLASS APART.

THOUGHTFUL PLANNING, SUSTAINABLE DESIGN AND METICULOUS EXECUTION IS OUR GROUP’S FOUNDATION.

THROUGH OUR PASSION FOR EXCELLENCE AND DEDICATION TO CUSTOMER SATISFACTION, WE AIM TO TRANSFORM THE RESIDENTIAL CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY AND SET THE STANDARD FOR LUXURY LIVING, TO REALIZE ALL THAT OUR CLIENTS HAVE EVER DREAMT OF.

Our Journey

SAFALYA PANORAMA - (2022)

Be among the first to own 4-side open apartments in Kalali. Experience the element of exclusivity in a space shared between only 52 families. Be mesmerised by the serenity that 75% perimeter exposure to the sky, has to offer. A classic, sleek design embroidered with nearness to nature.

BOUGANVILLA 108 - (2022)

Located in the emerging neighbourhood of Ankhol, Waghodia Road, these 4 BHK Triplex Bungalows are a bliss in itself. Offering contemporary lifestyle, with greenery by its side, Bouganvilla is the paradigm of a perfect balance between city and nature, between bustle and calm.

SAFALYA Rise - (2019)

Shining at the heart of Kalali, on the 30 mtr wide ring road is the exquisite Safalya Rise. Tailored according to one’s taste, RISE offers beautiful and affordable luxury like no other.

Safalya Arcade - (2017)

With an eye-catching infrastructure, contemporary elevation and stunning exteriors, Safalya Arcade is a sight one cannot overlook. Located on the 75 mtr Dabhoi Road and 18 mtr road junction, it’s at a distance of just 1 km. from Kapurai Chowkdi. Ideal for shops, showrooms and offices, it’s a place to let your business bloom.

SAFALYA BLISS - (2017)

An affordable, premium-quality housing, Safalya Bliss provides for an elegant lifestyle with plush amenities. At a prime residential location, it’s a spacious and ideal living space.

KUNJ RIVERSCAPE - (2011)

Developed by the banks of the river Dhadhar, off Dabhoi Road, Kunj Riverscape is a quick 30 minutes’ drive from Vadodara. These are elegant retreat homes, facing the river that give a chance to let loose, relax and soak in the goodness of nature. A haven from the chaos of daily life, it’s a provision of escape from the tiring reality as the river soothes one.
