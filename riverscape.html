Kunj Riverscape

RETREAT TO REJUVENATION

The Kunj Riverscape has been built on the banks of the Dhadhar river, off Dabhoi road. Only a 30-minute drive from Vadodara, these are retreat homes, providing the much-needed quality and rest time. Surrounded by the magnificent views of the river and the lush green environment, this 2 BHK living space is perfect for your family and an amazing escape from the stressful daily life. The Riverscape beckons you to live a life you have never lived, the one where you live to the fullest, appreciating nature, your loved ones and yourself. A time to regale, recharge and rejuvenate.
