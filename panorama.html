Safalya Panorama

LIVE THE VIEW

Be among the first ones to own 4-side open apartments in Kalali. With every home opening outward, the Safalya Panorama lets you be in tune with nature. The campus being shared between only 52 families highlights the emphasis on exclusivity. The straight lines with smooth curved edges create a delightful contrast which add a touch of class to this stylish piece of craft. Cornered by two 18 mtr roads, your home will be close to all the urban utilities and at the same time will also be away from the chaos of the city. With 75% perimeter exposed to the sky, a feeling of tranquillity is given.
